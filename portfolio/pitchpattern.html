pitch pattern




ABOUT:

Pitch Pattern is a step sequencer based off of Gregory Taylor's Step By Step Adventures In Sequencing With MaxMSP.

This patch uses a mic input to detect the pitch using retune~. It converts it to a MIDI note for the piano scroll, and sets the fundatmental pitch/first step of the sequencer. The users are able to change the pattern as well as other parameters, such as starting step, max# of steps, note value, and other audio processing controls.

Made with MAX MSP.
